About Me

Based in Casablanca, Morocco, I began my career in Data Analytics, working with data, dashboards, and business insights. While I enjoyed solving problems through analysis, I realized my true passion was elsewhere: creating worlds, telling stories, and building experiences that people can explore and enjoy. That realization led me to pursue my dream of becoming a Game Artist and Game Developer. Today, I focus on Environment Art, Level Design, World Building, and Game Design combining creativity with the structured problem-solving skills I developed as a data analyst.

I enjoy transforming ideas into immersive game spaces, designing environments that tell stories, and crafting gameplay experiences that feel both engaging and memorable. Whether I'm building a stylized world in Blender, designing a level in Unreal Engine, or collaborating on a game project, I'm constantly learning and pushing myself to grow as a creator. My journey from Data Analytics to Game Development has given me a unique perspective: the ability to blend analytical thinking with artistic vision. Now, I'm focused on turning that combination into a career in the game industry and contributing to projects that inspire players around the world.
