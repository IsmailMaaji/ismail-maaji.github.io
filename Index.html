About Me

Based in Casablanca, Morocco, I began my career in Data Analytics, working with data, dashboards, and business insights. While I valued the problem-solving aspect of the field, I gradually discovered a stronger passion for creating interactive worlds, telling stories, and designing experiences that players can explore and connect with. This shift led me to pursue game development and real-time 3D art.

Today, I focus on 3D art, game development, and interactive digital experiences,, exploring every stage of the creative pipeline—from modeling, texturing, and lighting to environment creation, animation, rendering, and game integration. I enjoy turning ideas into engaging visual experiences, whether I'm creating assets in Blender, building worlds in Unreal Engine, experimenting with AI-assisted workflows, or collaborating on game projects. My transition from Data Analytics to game development has given me a unique perspective, combining analytical thinking with creativity to solve problems and bring ideas to life. As a versatile creator, I'm continuously learning, expanding my skills across multiple disciplines, and challenging myself with new technologies and workflows. My goal is to grow within the game industry, contribute wherever I'm needed, and help create memorable experiences that inspire players around the world.
